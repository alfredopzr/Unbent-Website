ABOUT UNBENT

Unbent is a company focused on delivering Virtual Reality Soft and Hard Skill assessments that address skill and bias preferences, which can impact company branding and cost, roughly $240,000 per employee. It seeks to disrupt the traditional flow of pre-screening, which currently uses Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and filters out roughly 75% of qualified candidates using key word searches and instead, screen based on skill and biased decision preferences. With roughly 46% of new hires deemed as failures according to Harvard Business Review, and 98% of companies dependent on a inefficient ATS system, we seek to unbend the hiring process and get the right candidate to you in an unbiased way.

WHAT UNBENT PROVIDES

Organizations using Unbent fundamentally improve and streamline the way their hiring process works. Unbent reimagines how job applicants engage with hiring departments. Unbent provides the tools necessary to unlock the potential of any modern organization.
